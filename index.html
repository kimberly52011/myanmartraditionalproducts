Traditional Products

Products that have been historically recognized or produced according to traditional methods of production are called traditional products. Myanmar traditional products are Tat Nat Khar , Yoon , Myanmar Sand Paintings ,Longyi , Pathein Umbrellea and etc.

Our Aim

We want to show how our traditional products are valuable and beautiful. We are 'Burmese' so we have know about our traditional products. We also have to respect our traditional products too.

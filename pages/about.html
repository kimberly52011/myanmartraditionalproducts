About us

This website serves both foreign visitors and Burmese individuals who are unaware about Myanmar Traditional Products. We want to promote our Myanmar Traditional Products which is very valuable and beautiful. You will see a variety of materials, artwork, and designs. Our traditional products from Myanmar originated in different eras and cultures. We wish to demonstrate that our traditional products are unique and our treasure, while some other countries have copied. We wish to highlight the creativity of our Burmese people.
